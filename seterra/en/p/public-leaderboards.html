New: Leaderboards For Custom Quizzes

January 20, 2020

We have just added a new feature for our Seterra members: the ability to add public leaderboards to custom quizzes.


Just check the box…




…and everyone who has access to the custom quiz can put their name (max 12 characters) on the leaderboard!

A Seterra membership costs only 1 USD per month. All pledges are charged on the first of each calendar month, and you are free to cancel your pledge at any time.

Please note that, when you create a custom quiz, you can share it with anyone you wish, by sending them the link; they don't have to be Seterra members.

The top 100 scores are displayed on the leaderboard.

If you are a teacher, you might want to se ALL the scores that your students have posted, not just the top 100. You can do that by going to the "My Account" page (click your email address in the top right corner to get there), and find the custom game in the list. Click the "Scores" link to see a list of all scores! The list is sortable (click on the headers) and searchable.

If you have privacy concerns about the students seeing each others scores, there is also a checkbox that anonymizes the names on the leaderboards.
