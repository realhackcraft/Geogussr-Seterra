Seterra Top Scores


People frequently ask us what the best scores for certain Seterra games are. There are no global high score lists in Seterra, but, if you are a Seterra supporter, your scores are recorded and you can see personal high score lists for each game.

Supporter Scores

We've had a look at the high scores that have been recorded by our supporters, in order to find the best ones. Here are the current best supporter scores for the most popular Seterra games!

Full supporter high score list >>

If you want your scores recorded and a chance to get a spot on the list above, become a Seterra supporter!

We will not name any players without their permissions, but if you know that one of these is your score and want your name on the list, email us at info@seterra.com.

Youtube Scores

Another way to record your scores is to capture the screen while playing and post the gameplay on YouTube. You can also post them on Speedrun.com. There is even a Discord channel where you can discuss Seterra Speedrunning.
