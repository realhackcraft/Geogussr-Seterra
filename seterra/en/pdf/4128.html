Oceania: Countries and Territories Printables - Map Quiz Game

Teaching the geography of Oceania is possibly one of the most difficult regions for teachers. This downloadable PDF map of the region can be a helpful resource to get students to learn all 23 countries in the region. There is a labeled the map of Oceania that can be used as a study resource as well as a blank map of the region that can be used for quizzes.

You can also practice online using our online map quizzes.
