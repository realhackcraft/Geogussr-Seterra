Europe Geography Games

The massive northwestern peninsula of Eurasia is better known as the continent of Europe. It begins in the east with the Russian Ural Mountains and its western edge is defined by the Atlantic Ocean. To the southeast is Asia, and on the other side of the Mediterranean Sea to the south lies Africa. Seterra has a collection of engaging geography quizzes that will help you learn the European countries, capitals, flags, and more.

From Iceland in the far north to Cyprus in the south, Europe offers a sprawling landscape of fascinating geological facts. By providing quizzes about the entire continent and other quizzes that cover specific countries and regions, this collection lets you focus on just the part of Europe youre interested in, and all the map quizzes are customizable. There are also quizzes on European flags, a multiple-choice quiz on the capitals, and a quiz on Europes physical features, just to name a few. Use this collection of engaging study aids and let Seterra help you learn everything you need to know about European geography.
