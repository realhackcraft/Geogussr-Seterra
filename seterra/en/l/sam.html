South America Geography Games

The formation of the Isthmus of Panama united North and South America around 3 million years ago, and while they’re occasionally referred to as a single supercontinent, they are very different from each other geographically. Seterra has a collection of engaging geography quizzes that will help you learn the South American countries, capitals, flags, and more.

From Lake Maracaibo in the far north to Tierra Del Fuego in the southern tip, there are a lot of details about South American to memorize. By providing quizzes about the entire continent and other quizzes that cover specific countries and regions, this collection lets you focus on just the part of South America you’re interested in, and all the map quizzes are customizable. There’s also a quiz on South American flags, one covering the continent’s notable physical features, a multiple-choice quiz on the capitals, and printable study materials. Use this collection of engaging study aids and let Seterra help you learn everything you need to know about South American geography.
