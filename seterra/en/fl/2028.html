If you know that 1848 is the year Wisconsin was admitted to the Union, you’ll have an advantage in identifying that state’s flag. Ohio's flag is another easy one—its swallowtail shape makes it the only non-rectangular U.S. state flag. There’s something noteworthy about each of these flags, and this quiz game will help you memorize those features. Whether it’s the gold torch on Indiana’s flag, Rhode Island’s anchor, or the palm tree dominating the flag of South Carolina, you’ll be able to recognize the most notable flag elements with ease, so identifying the flags of all the eastern U.S. states will be a breeze!
