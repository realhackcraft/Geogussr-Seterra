This map quiz challenges you to identify the six districts of Belize. Cayo District in the west part of Belize is the largest district by area and the second-most populous. The district with the highest population is Belize District, the area first settled by Europeans, who developed estates along the rivers and creeks. Toledo District in the south is the least developed region, featuring rainforests, caves, coastal lowland plains, and offshore cays. By collecting facts about Belize and working with this map quiz, you can memorize the country's districts with ease!
