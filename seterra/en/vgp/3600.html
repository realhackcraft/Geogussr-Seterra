Here's a map quiz that challenges you to identify all 14 districts of the Ivory Coast. These districts are the first-level administrative subdivisions of the country. Two of the districts, Yamoussoukro and Abidjan, are autonomous districts and, unlike the other 12, are not further divided into regions. Yamoussoukro became the official capital of the Ivory Coast in 1983, but the former capital Abidjan retains some political functions. The 12 nonautonomous districts are further split into regions, departments, and smaller administrative divisions. By exploring details that make each district distinct and using this map quiz, you'll find it easy to memorize the 14 districts of the Ivory Coast!
