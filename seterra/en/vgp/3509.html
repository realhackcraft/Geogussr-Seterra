Georgia is divided into 159 counties and, with the help of this quiz game, memorizing them is going to be easy. Picking up a few state facts will help too. For example, Bartow County is the location of the Etowah Indian Mounds, a prehistoric site that was inhabited by Native Americans from 1000 A.D. to 1550 A.D. Atlanta is the capital of Georgia, the seat of Fulton County, and home to the Dixie Coca-Cola Bottling Company Plant, where the first Coca-Cola was bottled. By collecting facts about Georgia and working with this map quiz, you can memorize the state's 159 counties in no time!
