Overseas France is the term applied to all the French territories outside the European continent. This map quiz game will help you learn the location of all of them, including island territories in the Atlantic, Pacific, and Indian oceans, French Guiana in South America, and a few other small holdings. There are exotic tourist magnets like French Polynesia and the beautiful island of Martinique, and equally exotic places that no one has heard of, like Clipperton Island, a tiny, uninhabited coral atoll in the eastern Pacific Ocean near the coast of Central America. Big or small, well-known or obscure, the territories of Overseas France will be easy to memorize with the help of this engaging map quiz.
