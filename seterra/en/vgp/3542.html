Can you identify all 254 counties in Texas? This quiz game will make it easy and learning a few state facts will help too. In Harris County, you can find the USS Texas, a battleship that was used in both World Wars. The Spindletop Gusher in Jefferson County is a landmark commemorating the historic oil boom in Texas that occurred in the early 1900s. Galveston County is home to the Strand Historic District, the jewel of Texas' premier beach city, Galveston. Every county in Texas has at least one interesting landmark—if you explore the state and work with this map quiz, you'll be able to locate all this state's 254 counties in no time.
