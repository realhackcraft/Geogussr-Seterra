Can you identify the 6 districts of Cyprus? This quiz game will make it easy and learning a few facts about Cyprus will help too. The Limassol District contains the buried ancient city of Amathus with ruins of the Byzantine period and a tomb dating to the 7th century BC. Paphos District in the western part of Cyprus features the Akamas peninsula, part of which is a green sea turtle sanctuary. Cape Greco in the Famagusta District is popular among tourists, featuring a protected coastal nature park. If you learn some memorable details about Cyprus and work with this map quiz, you'll be able to locate all the country's districts in no time.
