Can you identify the US Presidents going back to 1929? This quiz game will make it easy and learning a few presidential facts will help too. Bill Clinton was president in October 1994 when the White House's first website went online. Before serving as the 39th President, Jimmy Carter was a peanut farmer. Ronald Reagan, the 40th president, was a popular movie star. In 1939 in a broadcast from the World's Fair, Franklin D. Roosevelt was the first president to appear on television. By collecting memorable details about the US Presidents and working with this quiz, you can memorize these relatively recent US leaders with ease!
