Can you name all 67 counties in Florida? This quiz game, along with learning a few state facts, will help. For example, St. Augustine, on the Atlantic coast, was founded in 1565 by Spanish explorers, making it the oldest continuously inhabited European-established settlement in the contiguous United States. It's also the county seat of St. Johns County! Beautiful Key West, in Monroe County, is the location of the Ernest Hemingway Home and Museum; it's the former residence of the famous author and a haven for approximately 45 polydactyl (six-toed) cats. Explore Florida and let this map quiz help you learn the state's 67 counties fast!
