Here's a map quiz that challenges you to identify the diverse local government areas (LGAs) of Victoria, a state in southeast Australia. Gilgandra Shire is a local government area located on the Castlereagh River. Glenelg is a coastal suburb of Adelaide. Shellharbour is a residential area built around the small recreational harbor, Shell Harbour. There are interesting details about Victoria that will help you memorize these areas—if you commit some to memory and work with this map quiz, you'll be able to identify the LGAs of Victoria in no time.
