This map quiz can help you locate the home city of every team in the National Football League (NFL). Los Angeles boasts two football teams, the Chargers, and the Rams, while 3000 miles to the east in Boston, New England Patriots fans stake their claim to football supremacy. There's the Seattle Seahawks and, on the opposite coast, the Miami Dolphins. You'll have to span the contiguous US to identify each team's turf, but if you work with this fun NFL quiz, you'll be able to memorize the locations of all 30 NFL team cities in no time!
