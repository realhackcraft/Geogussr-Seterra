Scandinavia consists of the five northernmost countries in Europe. They have a shared heritage dating back to the Vikings of the Middle Ages, during which time they had settlements throughout northern Europe, Greenland, and even Canada. Today, Scandinavian countries have the highest living standards in the world. Interestingly, they also have the world’s highest rates of coffee consumption.
