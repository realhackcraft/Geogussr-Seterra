There are about 350 official Italian wine varieties. That's because so many different varieties of grapes grow in Italy; some say the number is over 2,000. It makes sense, because Italy is such a geographically diverse country. This map quiz game will help you explore the northern regions like Trentino Alto-Adige and the Sicilian region in the extreme southern part of the country. Located near the middle of Italy, Tuscany is where Chianti is made, one of the most popular types of red wine in the world. The wine regions of Italy make for an engaging topic of study, and this quiz game helps you explore them and memorize the location of each region.
