Guyana's ten regions are easy to memorize when you use this map quiz. The Barima-Waini region is named for its two main rivers. The inland region of Upper Demerara-Berbice is home to Guyana's principal bauxite deposits. The Cuyuni-Mazaruni region is rich with gold and diamonds. Memorable geographical details about the country will make it easy to learn the regions of Guyana, and this map quiz will help.
