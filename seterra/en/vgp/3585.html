Can you identify these 99 local government areas (LGAs) of New South Wales? This quiz game will make it easy and learning a few facts about this part of Australia will help too. New South Wales, a state located on the east coast of Australia, is home to the world-class capital city, Sydney. In the northern part of Dungog Shire is rugged, hilly terrain, but it flattens out in the southern part of Dungog. Liverpool Plains, a region of valuable agricultural land, has a unique topography, with steep hills dotting the otherwise flat area. If you explore New South Wales and work with this map quiz, you'll be able to identify all these LGAs in no time.
