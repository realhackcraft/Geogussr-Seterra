This map quiz can help you locate the home city of every team in the English Premier League, the top-level football league in the UK. In the north, there's the Newcastle United Football Club, which was founded in 1892. Far south is the Brighton & Hove Albion Football Club, which is typically referred to simply as Brighton. Several teams are clustered in the Greater London region of the UK, including Brentford, Tottenham Hotspur, and Chelsea. Work with this fun English Premier League quiz and you'll be able to memorize the locations of all 20 UK Premier League team cities in no time!
