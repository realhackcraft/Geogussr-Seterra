The Canary Islands are part of Spain, but they're located way down near Morocco. In fact, tectonically speaking, the islands belong to the African Plate. The Canaries are one of the most remote regions that are considered part of Europe, and you can use this engaging study aid to memorize them. Over 12 million people visit Mount Teide on the island of Tenerife every year; it's the third tallest volcano in the world and a fascinating place to explore. See if you can find Tenerife on the map. With the help of this quiz game, you'll be able to identify all 7 of the Canary Islands, plus two of their major cities.
