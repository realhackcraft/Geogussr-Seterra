The Seterra App for iOS and Android


There is a mobile version of Seterra too! The Seterra app runs on all iOS and Android phones and tablets. Click one of the banners on the right to download the app from the Apple App Store or from Google Play! The Seterra app offers high score lists to keep track of your progress and offline play!

Support the Development of Seterra!

Upgrade to Pro Support Seterra and make it possible for us to create an even better map quiz site! If you create a Pro account, you get:
  • No ads on the Seterra website!
  • High score lists! Keep track of your own progress on each map quiz!
  • Add custom locations to the map quizzes!
  • Add leaderboards to your custom quizzes, where your students or friends can register their scores.
  • Post assignments to Google Classroom.

Anatomy and Science Quizzes!

Seterra can help you learn about more than geography--we offer a collection of engaging science quizzes too! There are quizzes on the human anatomy, covering the skeletal system, major muscles, organs, and more. You can learn the periodic table, and play helpful quiz games about general science topics as well, like cloud types, animal and plant cells, and the parts of a flower. Click here to check out our science quizzes! Available in English, Swedish, German and Portuguese.

More Geography Games? Try Geoguessr!

Imagine being dropped in a completely random place on Earth. You don't know where you are, but you see some clues. You can have that experience on our partner site, Geoguessr.com! Go for a walk, have a look around, and then click the map to guess where on Earth you are!<
